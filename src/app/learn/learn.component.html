Serrate

Entire

Leaves that are neither toothed, lobed, or compound, i.e. simple with smooth margins.

Strongly wavy margins with shallowly rounded divisions within the same plane of the blade

Forward pointing teeth with each tooth (serration) having smaller serrations on it

Spinose

Has spines along leaf margins or has tiny spines at nodes on stems. Often these spines occur on the margin where the leaf veins terminate at serrations at the leaf margin.

Serrulate

Minutely serrated. rough - of the margin of a leaf shape; having the edge cut or fringed or scalloped.
